---
title: 'Enterprise'
description: 'This new feature allows you to showcase all your projects and availabilities in a single map interface. Here is how you can build it.'
---

<Note>In Planpoint Enterprise, you can integrate projects from a single account or many different accounts (by invitation)</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Go to Enterprises">
    In your Planpoint dashboard click on **Enterprises**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/hPX8Kfw8/enterprise-1.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2: Create your Enterprise project">
    To create you enterprise project, click on **new enterprise**. Assign a name for the enterprise and click on **save and create**.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/Gm8jxq1s/enterprise-2.jpg" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/cHHCfkVp/enterprise-3.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3: Enterprise dashboard">
    Once you create your project or projects, you can observe them displayed in your enterprise dashboard. Then click on **Settings** to begin editing your enterprise project.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/X7QVkkg3/enterprise-4.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4: Edit your new Enterprise project">
    Here, you can customize your enterprise project. You will be able to add your Enterprise image, edit the project's name, assign the embed options and prices, set the availabilities, layouts, and colors you want to use.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/sX98g07L/enterprise-1.png" />
    </Frame>

    To finalize the project settings, you need to organize everything related to geolocation. Here you can select the map style, the target marker, the map point color and add directions if you wish.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/sD1nk26q/enterprise-2.png" />
    </Frame>
    
    To choose the map to be displayed on the enterprise dashboard, you must set the desired view you want using the map on the right side. Then click on **use this view**. You can also use the icons on the upper right corner of the map to adjust your map view. 
  
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/J4gTMWSn/enterprise-3.jpg" />
    </Frame>
    <Note>You can choose to turn the initial zoom-in and dynamic centering on or off for your enterprise project</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 5: Add group projects">
    Go to the **groups** tab to add any group project you have. 
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/yYSGJdm9/enterprise-4.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  
    Once there, simply click on **add group** and select the group projects you want to include in your enterprise.

    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/yNnpqSKq/enterprise-5.jpg" />
    </Frame>

    <Frame caption="Select the desired groups projects and then click Add to Enterprise">
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/c128ZZnP/enterprise-11.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 6: Add projects">
    Go to the **projects** tab to add any project you want.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/fLL2MTWV/enterprise-6.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  
    <Frame caption="Select the desired projects and then click Add to Enterprise">
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/qB1xKg5Y/enterprise-13.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 7: External projects">
    You can also add external projects by searching for them by their name. Simply go to the **External** tab, type the name of the project, choose the project and send a request. After the request is accepted by the project's owner, you will be able to add it to your enterprise.

    <Frame caption="Pending requests will be visible at the bottom of the page">
      <img src="https://i.postimg.cc/QCKfW4Ts/enterprise-7.jpg" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
<Tip>You can also manage the members of your team who can administer or edit your enterprise</Tip>
